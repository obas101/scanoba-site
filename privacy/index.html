Privacy Policy

Scanoba by Tegribas Global Limited — last updated July 2026

No account required No document uploads Camera permission only

Scanoba is a document scanner app built to work entirely on your device. This policy explains, in plain language, exactly what does and doesn't happen with your data.

What Scanoba does not do

  • Scanoba does not require you to create an account or sign in.
  • Scanoba does not upload your scanned documents, images, or PDFs to any server. Everything you scan is saved only in this app's private storage on your device.
  • Scanoba does not read, share, or sell your documents to anyone, including us.
  • Scanoba never watermarks your exports, on any plan.

Permissions Scanoba requests

Scanoba only ever asks for one permission up front:

  • Camera — used to scan documents. Photos are processed on your device to detect edges, correct perspective, and build your PDF. They are never transmitted anywhere.

Two more permissions are requested only at the exact moment you choose to use a related feature:

  • Photo library — only if you tap "Save to camera roll" for a scanned page.
  • Biometrics (Face ID / fingerprint) — only if you turn on Document Lock for a specific file. Your biometric data is handled entirely by your device's operating system; it never reaches Scanoba or leaves your phone.

Local storage and document encryption

Your scanned documents, folders, and settings are stored locally using your device's own storage. If you turn on Document Lock for a file, that PDF is encrypted at rest (AES-256) with a key held in your device's secure hardware-backed keystore (Android Keystore / iOS Keychain) — not by us.

Third-party services we rely on

Scanoba is free to use with no watermarks. To keep it that way, the free tier shows ads, and the Pro tier is sold as a subscription. Both involve a small number of well-known third-party services, described here for transparency:

  • Google AdMob — shows a banner ad on the Library screen for free-tier users only. Ads are never shown while scanning, reviewing, or saving a document. AdMob may collect device and advertising identifiers under Google's own privacy policy.
  • RevenueCat — manages Pro subscription status across devices so you can restore a purchase by email. RevenueCat receives purchase/subscription receipt data from the App Store or Google Play — never your scanned documents. See RevenueCat's privacy policy.
  • Apple App Store / Google Play — handle all payment details directly. Scanoba never sees or stores your card information.

Children's privacy

Scanoba is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from them.

Deleting your data

Since everything lives on your device, deleting your data is entirely in your hands: delete a document or folder from within the app, or uninstall the app to remove everything at once.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we'll update the date at the top of this page. Continued use of Scanoba after a change means you accept the updated policy.
